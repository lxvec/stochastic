Mathematical Definition

Martingale: A process {Sₜ}ₜ≥₀ is a martingale with respect to filtration {Fₜ} and measure ℚ if:

  • Sₜ is Fₜ-measurable for all t
  • E^ℚ[|Sₜ|] < ∞
  • E^ℚ[Sₜ | Fₛ] = Sₛ for all s ≤ t

In Finance: Under the risk-neutral measure ℚ, the discounted stock price is a martingale.
