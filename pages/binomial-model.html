Mathematical Setup

Binomial Tree Model:

  • Sₜ₊₁ = Sₜ × u with probability p (real-world)
  • Sₜ₊₁ = Sₜ × d with probability 1-p (real-world)
  • Under risk-neutral measure ℚ: p* = (e^(rΔt) - d)/(u - d)

European Call Option:

  • Payoff at maturity T: C_T = max(S_T - K, 0)
  • Price at t: C_t = E^ℚ[e^(-r(T-t)) C_T | F_t]
