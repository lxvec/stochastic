Quick Reference

Euler–Maruyama scheme:

Xt+Δt = Xt + κ(θ−Xt)Δt + σ(Wt+Δt−Wt)

Wt+Δt − Wt ~ N(0, Δt) i.i.d.

Slope on chart = (Xt+Δt − Xt) / Δt

κ > 0: mean-reverting toward θ

κ < 0: explosive (diverges from θ)

κ = 0: pure diffusion (random walk)
